COMMERCIAL RAIN GUTTERS AND COLLECTORS

Commercial gutters are shop fabricated to any size required for the job. The Original Roofing Company also has a complete metal shop capable of designing any size gutter. Collection boxes are often used as a catch basin for water filtrating off of flat roofs. Collection boxes can be shop fabricated in any size or shape.

Available products include:

  • Galvanized gutters and collection boxes in any gauge thickness
  • Copper gutters and collection boxes
  • Patina finishes available for copper or galvanized products

Decorative styles and details available in collection boxes
